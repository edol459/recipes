Starbucks Knockoffs

RECIPES

Pink Drink

Ingredients

  • ½ cup white grape juice
  • 1 cup hot boiling water
  • 4 packs acai berry tea
  • Agave nectar
  • Fresh strawberries
  • 1 to 2 cups of unsweetened coconut milk

Salted Caramel Mocha

Ingredients

  • For homemade caramel:
  • 5 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1⁄3 cup cold water
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons heavy cream
  • A large pinch of salt to taste
  • For mocha:
  • 2 tablespoons homemade Starbucks salted caramel sauce
  • ½ cup of chocolate milk
  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream

Iced Matcha Latte

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon Matcha
  • 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Syrup
  • 1 handful of ice cubes
